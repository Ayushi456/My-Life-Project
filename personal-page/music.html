Favorite Music

1. Lana Del Rey

All of Lana Del Rey's music has a story which I like. I like that she has both upbeat and more mellow songs. Her music always has a good beat and melody to it.

2. Melanie Martinez

Melanie Martinez has a very creative take on pop music. Her entire album is one big story which I think is very creative and each song tells a story in a unique way. She uses a lot of metaphors and personification in her music.

3. Years and Years

This is one of my favorite bands. They use a lot of good beats in their music. I really like the lead singer's voice. I especially like their song's "Desire" and "Take Shelter"
